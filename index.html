Frequently asked
Questions

How often should I water my indoor plants?

Most indoor plants thrive with once-a-week watering, but it varies by plant type. Check specific care instructions.

What's the best lighting for my succulents?

Plants are easy way to add color & aestetics to your spaceSucculents prefer bright, indirect sunlight. Place them near a sunny window for optimal growth.

How can I revive a wilting plant?

Ensure proper watering and drainage, trim dead leaves, and consider repotting if root-bound.

How can I prevent overwatering my plants?

Allow the top inch of soil to dry before watering and use well-draining pots and soil.
